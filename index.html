Specialist Firearms Training

Mission

Specialist Firearms Training delivers unique firearm skills in an exceptional learning environment by a qualified and experienced instructor. Specialist Firearms Training tuition enables operators to shoot at a higher skill level. Operators leave Specialist Firearms Training with a wider competancy and understanding of firearms skills, tactics, shooting techniques and safety.

Types of Firearms

  • AR-15 Tactical Carbine 5.56
  • Winchester SXP Tactical 12G
  • Remington 870 Tactical 12G
  • Remington 700 SPS .308
  • M+P Smith and Wesson .40 Cal
  • Beretta PX4 Storm Pistol 9mm

Price

$95.00 / hour
  • Four hour minimum applies
  • All range instruction is live fire and includes use of firearm(s), range hire for (5) hours and 200 rounds 9mm / .40 SW, all other calibre ammunition will be at discounted cost to operators whilst under instruction.

All operators will require possession of a current (PAL) Restricted / Non-Restricted Federal Firearms Licence and sign a waiver. It is legal to shoot on a gun range in Canada without a Firearms Licence (PAL) provided the individual is under the immediate supervision, care and control of a current firearms licence (PAL) holder. I will review individuals not in possession of a firearms licence on a case by case basis.
